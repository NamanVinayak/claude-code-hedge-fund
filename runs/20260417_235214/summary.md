## Concept Reference

- **Swing trade**: A multi-day to multi-week directional bet that tries to catch one price swing, not a long-term investment.
- **Entry price**: The level where the model wants to start the position. Think of it as your "buy alarm."
- **Target price**: Where you take profits.
- **Stop-loss**: Your bailout level if the idea is wrong. Non-negotiable.
- **Risk-reward ratio (R/R)**: Target-distance ÷ stop-distance. 2:1 means you're risking $1 to try to make $2. The PM refuses any setup below 2:1.
- **ADX**: A measure of trend strength. Below 20 = no trend. Above 25 = real trend. Above 40 = strong/possibly over-extended.
- **EMA alignment**: Short-term EMA above long-term EMA = uptrend; the reverse = downtrend.
- **Confidence**: The Head Trader's estimate of how likely this setup plays out. 70% means "fairly sure, not certain."
