# AAPL — Thesis

## TL;DR

**Prior thesis falsified on gross margin and tariff read.** The April 2026 thesis stated Apple was a "wait and watch" until Q2 FY2026 earnings resolved the tariff-margin binary and produced a clean breakout above $263. Both conditions have now triggered: Q2 gross margin came in at 49.3% (record — not the collapse to 2–3% the bear case feared), and the stock broke out above $276.11 on 2.08x volume on April 30. The "wait and watch" stance is now superseded by a conditional post-earnings drift setup. Bull thesis is active but entry-disciplined — the system is not chasing at $282; it is waiting for a pullback to $274–278. CEO Tim Cook's announced departure (September 1, 2026) and ADX still below 25 are the new primary caveats.

## What would change my mind
