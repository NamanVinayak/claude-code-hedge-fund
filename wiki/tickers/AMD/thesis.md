## Bull case

**AI infrastructure tailwinds are durable.** Data Center revenue hit $5.38B in Q4 2025 (+39% YoY), and Q1 2026 consensus projects ~$9.8B total revenue (+32.3% YoY) with EPS +35.4% YoY (source: web_research/AMD.json, run 20260501_132340). Hyperscaler demand is structural, not cyclical.

**The Meta deal is transformational.** Meta committed a $60B infrastructure deal covering AMD GPUs, Venice-architecture CPUs, and custom silicon co-engineering (source: web_research/AMD.json, run 20260415_110848). AMD server CPU market share crossed 40% in early 2026; Intel's Q1 blowout (+22% Data Center & AI revenue) is a positive read-through for AMD EPYC (source: web_research/AMD.json, 24/7 Wall St. 2026-04-29).

**TSMC 2nm first-mover advantage.** CEO Lisa Su confirmed AMD will be the first company on TSMC's 2nm process — a potential manufacturing moat against both Nvidia and Intel (source: web_research/AMD.json, run 20260415_110848).

**Analyst community net positive.** 34 analysts, 41% Strong Buy, 38% Buy, 21% Hold, 0% Sell. DA Davidson upgraded to Buy, PT raised $220→$375 (Apr 29). Susquehanna reiterated Buy, PT raised $300→$375. Melius Research targeting $380 (source: web_research/AMD.json, 20260501_132340). Note: avg PT of $296 is now below current price ($354), meaning even the bulls have limited published upside targets from here.

## Bear case

**Extreme valuation is a structural overhang.** P/E 135, P/S 18.5 (source: fundamentals_analyst_agent, signals_combined.json, run 20260501_132340). DCF intrinsic value implies 94% downside to market cap; owner earnings model implies 52% downside (source: valuation_analyst_agent, same run). The valuation agent was 100% bearish.

**Tariffs create genuine margin risk.** Trump's 25% tariff on advanced AI chips covers AMD's MI325X (enacted January 14, 2026). GPU/memory prices rising; multi-year hyperscaler contracts limit AMD's pass-through ability (source: prior web_research runs).

**Nvidia's moat has not narrowed.** Nvidia holds ~89% server GPU market share; custom silicon (Google TPU, Amazon Trainium) is an emerging structural threat to both (source: web_research/AMD.json, 20260501_132340).

**Insider selling is persistent.** CEO Lisa Su sold ~$16M in March 2026. Board-level ratio: 83 buys / 188 sells — 2:1 sellers (source: swing_catalyst_news signal, run 20260501_132340).

## What would change my mind

**Bull to neutral or bear:** Q1 earnings miss on Data Center revenue (below $5B), any hyperscaler contract cancellation, MI450 delay beyond Q3 2026, or tariff expansion.

**Bear to bull:** Nvidia supply shortage forcing hyperscalers to AMD at scale, MI450 benchmarks beating H200 equivalent, or tariff exemption for foundry customers.
